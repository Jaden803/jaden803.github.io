About us

We are an amazing burger joint located locally in Huntington Beach, California. What makes us different from others is our unique twist on our delicious foods. For example, we love to go ham on toppings. Our hot dogs are stuffed to perfection and our burgers are monsters! We also got the best shakes and sides. Come stop by our restaurant and tell us what you think!

What inspired us

Being born and raised in Southern California, we grew up surrounded by all kinds of cultures with fantastic foods. As time went by, a lot of the goto burger joints were blending into each other and became saturated. So, we wanted to stand out. We decided to take SoCal's most popular foods to the next level by thinking outside the box. We decided to use fresh ingredients and herbs to create an everlasting taste and combine them with already amazing-tasting food to create an even better experience.
